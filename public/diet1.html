Your Infant Is Underweight!!

Here Are The Dietary Recommendations


  • Breastmilk is the best balanced and healthy food for baby. It is also rich in fats and helps weight gain.
  • Eggs are great for weight gain.Egg yolk can be introduced in baby’s diet after 8 months. Doctors advice avoiding egg white till 1 year.
  • Khichdi is a wholesome meal of lentils and rice and is great for healthy weight gain. There are plenty of variations of khichdi too.
  • Potatoes and other starchy vegetables can be added in baby’s diet.
